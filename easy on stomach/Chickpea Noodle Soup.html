Chickpea Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 large carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 2 small cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/4 tsp. smoked paprika
  • 3/4 tsp. ground mustard
  • 3/4 tsp. onion powder Pinch cayenne pepper Kosher salt Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 c. low-sodium chicken stock
  • 6 oz. cream cheese
  • 6 c. small broccoli florets
  • 4 c. freshly grated sharp cheddar

Method

Step 1

In a medium saucepan, melt the butter. Add the carrot and garlic and cook, stirring until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Stir in paprika, ground mustard, onion powder, and cayenne. Pour in the chicken stock and bring to a boil. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 2

Add the broccoli and simmer until very tender, about 3 to 5 minutes. Add the cream cheese and cheddar and cook, stirring, until melted.

Step 3

Season with salt and pepper and serve warm. Nutrition (per serving): 516 calories, 23 g protein, 12 g carbohydrates, 5 g fiber, 3 g sugar, 43 g fat, 27 g saturated fat, 839 mg sodium
