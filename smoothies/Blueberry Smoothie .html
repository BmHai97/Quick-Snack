Blueberry Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 piece kombu, about 5x5 inches Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 c. fresh blueberries
  • 1 c. frozen blueberries
  • 1 tbsp. maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp. almond butter
  • 2 c. oat milk
  • 1 c. loosely packed baby spinach Pinch kosher salt

Method

Step 1

In a high-powered blender, combine all ingredients and blend until smooth.

Step 2

Top each bowl with fresh blueberries, granola, almonds, hemp seeds, and cinnamon before serving.
